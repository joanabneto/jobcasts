About us

If you're on this website, you — like us — probably have also wondered what some professions are really about. We started this project to try and find out how people perceive their job, what they like and don't like about it and how much importance they give to it in their lives. Our aim is not only to understand what some jobs entail, but also to create empathy between people.

We want to know how each one of us can contribute to making someone's job easier, even with little gestures. By getting to know what goes on behind the scenes, we can understand better the person in front of us. A small change in someone's approach can make a big difference in someone else's working day.

If you would like to be interviewed, know someone who would like to be interviewed or if you're really interested in a job that we haven't covered yet, please reach out to us and help us expand this project!
